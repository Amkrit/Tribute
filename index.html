Virat Kohli: The King of Cricket

About Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is an Indian cricketer and the current captain of the India national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time.

Achievements

  • Fastest to 10,000 runs in ODI cricket
  • Most centuries in international cricket as captain
  • ICC Cricketer of the Year (2017, 2018)

Inspirational Quotes

"I never think about what I'm going to get, I always think about what I'm going to give." Virat Kohli
